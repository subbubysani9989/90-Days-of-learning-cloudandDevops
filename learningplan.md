## 💡 My Understanding of DevOps & Cloud

DevOps is not just about tools — it is a culture and mindset where development and operations teams work together to build, test, deploy, and manage applications faster and more reliably using automation.

### Core Principles of DevOps:
• Collaboration over silos  
• Automation over manual work  
• Continuous improvement  
• Shared responsibility  
• Continuous testing, deployment, and monitoring  

Cloud Engineering is the process of building and managing applications, servers, storage, networking, and infrastructure on cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.
